spack.cmd.elide_list(line_list, max_num=10)¶

Takes a long list and limits it to a smaller number of elements, replacing intervening elements with ‘...’. For example:

elide_list([1,2,3,4,5,6], 4)

gives:

[1, 2, 3, '...', 6]
